About us

Indulge in opulence at Rovigo's premier dining destination! Seeking the epitome of luxury in Rovigo? Look no further! Nestled in the heart of the city, our renowned establishment has been tantalizing taste buds since 1939.

Experience the epitome of Turkish culinary excellence as we proudly serve the finest kebabs in all of Italy. Immerse yourself in a culinary journey like no other, where every dish is meticulously crafted to perfection.

Conveniently located in the bustling center of Rovigo, we eagerly await the opportunity to exceed your expectations, whether you dine in with us or prefer the comfort of your own home. Get ready to elevate your dining experience with us!
